Our hero FINCH M’APPELLE is a 27 year old ex-convict who got out of prison early for good behaviour. He lives in Bracknell, and two kilometres away is an children’s home, where his best friend JACK, a mechanical genius, lives. JACK is forced to work at Woking’s Top Pizzi, which is also right next to where Finch works, at Zizzi’s, consequently, they are best buddies. Searching for a killer, JACK and FINCH are traveling across a globe of adventure.
